Welcome to LocalFix

"Post a Task, Consider it Done."

About LocalFix

LocalFix is your go-to platform for connecting with skilled individuals in your community who can help with everyday tasks and jobs. Whether you need help with home repairs, gardening, tutoring, or any other service, LocalFix makes it easy to find reliable help quickly.

Our mission is to empower local talent and provide a seamless experience for users looking to get things done efficiently. Post your task, set your budget, and let the community take care of the rest!

What It Does?

LocalFix is a streamlined community bulletin board that connects people who need a hand with local helpers ready to work. It allows users to post specific service requests ranging from furniture assembly and tech support to grocery runs complete with a description and a set budget. Others can browse these live listings in real-time to find local opportunities that match their skills.

How It Works?

The platform operates on a simple, transparent "Post-and-Connect" model. A user submits a task through a straightforward form, which instantly populates the public task board. Interested helpers can then view the job details and use the provided contact information to reach out directly. Built with a lightweight PHP and MySQL backend, the site ensures that data is stored securely and displayed dynamically without unnecessary complexity.

Why it Exist?

In an era of expensive, corporate service apps, CampusTask was created to bring the focus back to community-level help. It exists to remove the barriers between neighbors, providing a free and accessible space where people can save time and others can earn extra income. By digitizing the traditional "paper flyer" board, it makes finding local help faster, more organized, and available to everyone.
